Why Custom WordPress?

Most WordPress Websites Underperform. Here's Why Yours Won't.

WordPress powers 43% of the internet — which means most WordPress websites look and function exactly like each other. A theme downloaded from ThemeForest, a page builder doing all the heavy lifting, and a site so loaded with unnecessary code that it takes 6 seconds to load on mobile. That is not a business asset. That is a digital liability.

We build WordPress differently. Every website we create starts with a blank canvas and ends with a custom-coded, precisely engineered solution that is tailored to your business, your brand, and your audience. No excess code. No unnecessary plugins. No compromises on speed, security, or functionality.

For businesses in the UK, where Google's Core Web Vitals scores directly impact rankings, and for Saudi businesses where mobile-first browsing dominates — the difference between a templated WordPress site and a custom-built one is the difference between a business tool and an embarrassment.

Our Process

How We Deliver WordPress Projects — Step by Step

01

STEP 1 — DISCOVERY & STRATEGY (Week 1)

We spend time understanding your business model, your audience, your competitors, and your technical requirements. We audit your existing site if applicable and identify what's working, what isn't, and what needs to be built. The output is a clear project brief that everyone on our team works from.

02

STEP 2 — ARCHITECTURE & UI/UX DESIGN (Weeks 1–2)

Before any code is written, we plan the information architecture — how the site is structured, how users navigate it, and what the conversion paths are. We then design the user interface in Figma, showing you exactly how every page will look on desktop and mobile before development begins.

03

STEP 3 — CUSTOM DEVELOPMENT (Weeks 2–5+)

Our WordPress developer builds the custom theme and any required functionality from scratch. We work in a staged development environment, run regular code reviews, and keep you updated at defined milestones. No surprises.

04

STEP 4 — CONTENT INTEGRATION, TESTING & QA (Weeks 5–6)

We integrate all content, run comprehensive cross-browser and cross-device testing, conduct a full performance audit, run a security review, and fix any issues identified. Nothing goes live until every element has been thoroughly tested.

05

STEP 5 — LAUNCH, TRAINING & POST-LAUNCH SUPPORT

We manage the launch, monitor the site closely in the days following go-live, and provide a handover session where we train you to manage content confidently. Post-launch support is available, and we'll handle any issues that arise immediately after launch at no extra charge.

FAQ

WordPress Questions We Get Asked Most

How long does a custom WordPress website take to build?
The timeline depends on the complexity and scale of the project. A professional business website typically takes 3 to 6 weeks. A complex WooCommerce store or a feature-heavy platform can take 8 to 16 weeks. We provide a specific timeline in every proposal.
What is the difference between a custom WordPress theme and a premium template?
A premium template is a design someone else built, carrying code you don't need, and impossible to truly make your own. A custom theme is built specifically for your brand from scratch — clean code, only the functionality you need. The performance difference is significant.
Can you migrate my existing website to WordPress?
Yes — we handle migrations from Wix, Squarespace, Magento, and other systems. We preserve all your content, maintain your URL structure to protect existing SEO rankings, and test thoroughly before and after the migration.
Do you build WordPress websites for businesses in Saudi Arabia?
Yes. We build bilingual (Arabic/English) WordPress websites including RTL support, Arabic typography, Saudi payment gateway integration, and local SEO considerations for Google Saudi Arabia.
Will my WordPress site be mobile-friendly and SEO-optimised?
Every WordPress site we build is mobile-first responsive and built with technical SEO best practices embedded from the ground up — clean code, structured data, and optimised page speeds.
What happens after my site goes live — do you offer support?
Yes. We offer ongoing maintenance retainers that cover core and plugin updates, security monitoring, backups, and technical support. You're also welcome to manage the site independently.
