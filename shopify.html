FAQ

Shopify Questions We Get Asked Most

How long does a custom Shopify store take to build?
A standard custom Shopify theme typically takes 4 to 8 weeks from design to launch. Stores with complex functionality, large product catalogues, or custom app development may take 8 to 16 weeks. We provide a specific timeline in every proposal.
Can you migrate my WooCommerce store to Shopify?
Yes — we handle full WooCommerce-to-Shopify migrations including products, variants, customer data, order history, and URL redirect mapping to preserve your SEO rankings. We have done this migration multiple times and have a structured process that minimises risk and disruption.
Do you offer Shopify development for businesses in Saudi Arabia?
Yes. We have built Shopify stores for Saudi brands, including Arabic language support, right-to-left (RTL) layout, and integration with Saudi and UAE-specific payment methods like Tamara and Tabby. We understand the Saudi e-commerce consumer and build accordingly.
What is the difference between Shopify and Shopify Plus?
Shopify Plus is Shopify's enterprise tier — it offers significantly more customisation of the checkout experience, advanced automation through Shopify Flow, B2B wholesale functionality, multi-store management, and dedicated support. We recommend Shopify Plus for brands doing over £1M/year in Shopify revenue or brands with complex B2B or multi-channel requirements.
Can you build a Shopify store that integrates with my existing CRM or ERP?
Yes. We regularly build custom integrations between Shopify stores and CRM systems (HubSpot, Zoho, Salesforce) and ERP systems (Odoo, SAP, custom solutions) using the Shopify API and middleware tools. This ensures your store data and business operations stay in sync automatically.
