1. What data we collect

We collect standard information such as your name, email address, phone number, and company details when you submit a query or request a quote through our integrated forms. We also automatically collect IP addresses and browser configurations for analytics purposes to ensure optimal site rendering.

2. How we use your data

Your provided personal data is utilised explicitly to fulfil your requests. For example, generating custom project proposals or responding to your enquiries within our promised 2-4 hour window. We do not use this data for unapproved marketing campaigns.

3. Who we share it with

SkyraSoft respects your privacy rigorously. We categorically do not sell or rent your data to any third-party brokers. Data is only shared with trusted technical sub-processors (like AWS or secure CRM tools) solely for delivering our services.

4. Data retention

We securely hold your personal data for precisely as long as is necessary to fulfil the agreed legal and contractual obligations. Quote request data that does not convert to an active project is securely destroyed within 12 months.

5. Your rights as a user (GDPR compliance)

Under UK and applicable KSA regulations, you have the right to access, rectify, or demand the deletion of any personal data we hold about you. You may also formally withdraw consent at any given time.

6. Cookie Policy

We deploy essential cookies to maintain site stability. Performance cookies (Google Analytics) are used to track anonymised user behaviour to improve UI. You may opt out of non-essential cookies via your browser configurations.

7. Contact Details

For formal data requests, please write directly to our designated compliance officer at privacy@skyrasoft.com.
